About the Show

Genre: Medical Drama, Mystery, Sarcasm

Years Aired: 2004 – 2012

Seasons: 8

“House M.D.” follows Dr. Gregory House, a brilliant, pill-popping misanthrope who solves baffling medical cases in between barbs at his coworkers.

Everybody lies—especially patients. But House’s uncanny diagnostic skills and total disregard for social norms often lead to the right solution… eventually.

Cast

  • Hugh Laurie as Dr. Gregory House
  • Lisa Edelstein as Dr. Lisa Cuddy
  • Omar Epps as Dr. Eric Foreman
  • Robert Sean Leonard as Dr. James Wilson
  • Jennifer Morrison as Dr. Allison Cameron
  • Jesse Spencer as Dr. Robert Chase

Fun Fact

Hugh Laurie’s American accent was so convincing, producers didn’t know he was British when they cast him.

Medical Accuracy

The show had medical advisors to ensure the diseases were portrayed realistically—though the timeline often got sped up.

Ratings & Awards

“House M.D.” was among the highest-rated TV shows worldwide, winning multiple Emmy Awards and Golden Globes.
